
Step 1: Prepare the Dataset
Sign Up on Roboflow:

Go to Roboflow and create an account.

Choose or Upload a Dataset:

Explore datasets in Roboflow Universe or upload your own images.

Annotate the Dataset:

If your dataset isn't labeled, annotate images by marking the faces. Roboflow provides an easy annotation tool.

Export the Dataset:

Export the dataset in a format compatible with your model (e.g., YOLOv8, TensorFlow). Copy the API key for accessing the dataset programmatically.
